About Me

In 2014 I moved to the Netherlands where I got the opportunity to intern at the U.S Embassy in the I.T Department. From a young age I have always loved working with technology. But this was my first time working with tech in a professional environment. My responsibilities were to solve tech related problems from troubleshooting computers tp fixing Blackberries. This started my passion for working on complex problems and the rush that comes from solving them. When I graduated high school in 2016 I decided to go to Belmont University where I studied economics and political science which I enjoyed but it was not my passion. My junior year I decided to take the leap to leave Belmont  to freelance creating websites. Working with clients to create websites using Wordpress. Doing this sparked a deeper desire to be able to create websites from the ground up. As a result I enrolled Nashville Software School. Which would give the skills to create my own websites and give me the chance to be a part of the Nashville tech community.
